Admission

Procedures & Requirements

  • STEPS INVOLVED

    Please note that all students applying to KANDIT STANDARD SCHOOL (except Pre-School students) will participate in a placement test and undergo an interview with the Head of the school. Once all considerations are made the applicant will procede to be admitted.

  • STEP ONE (1)

    In order to ensure a safe and happy school experience for all our young learners, we ask that parents/guidiance should Please submit the following materials to the school before a placement test can be taken:

  • STEP TWO (2)

    • The materials include:
    • One passport sized photograph
    • Copy of birth certificate or passport
    • Report Card from previous School
    • Immunization record (for Pre-School only)
    • Completed Application form

  • HOW TO PAY FEES

    Visit any branch of SINAPI ABA saving & Loans nation wide to make payment. After send the payment slip to the school's admission Office.
