About

HISTORICAL BACKGROUND

Kandit Standard is a young school which started in 2012 academic year. The name of the school was taken from the Bible verse Phillipians 4:13 which says... I can do all things through CHRIST who strengthens me. Kan do it was shortened to be kandit. The name Standard was choosen because we wanted the school to conform to the educational Standard and intenational Standard as well. The school initially started with two pupils namely; Josephine and Lois. The vision of the school is to unleash the potentials of the young people through quality education, which tackles moral, academics, character training and everything about the child.
