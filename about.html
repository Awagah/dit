About

HISTORICAL BACKGROUND

Established on August 28th, 2012, and registered with the Government of Ghana on May 27th, 2019, KANDIT Standard School is a thriving educational institution located in Bibiani-California, Western North Region of Ghana.
The school initially started with two pupils namely; Josephine and Lois. The name of the school was taken from the Bible verse Phillipians 4:13 which says... I can do all things through CHRIST who strengthens me. Kan do it was shortened to be kandit. The name Standard was choosen because we wanted the school to conform to the educational Standard and international Standard as well.

OUR MISSION

At KANDIT Standard School, we strive to become a center of excellence, unleashing the potential of young people through quality education and good ethics. Our core focus is to educate, develop, train, and mentor children to excel academically and positively impact society.


OUR VISION

With years of experience in the education sector, we aim to increase accessibility to educational opportunities for children in remote areas. We plan to expand our activities to educationally disadvantaged communities, leveraging our strengths in:

  • Experienced Staff
  • Well-Defined Vision and Mission
  • Innovative and Goal-Oriented Approach
  • Serene School Environment
